What is the film calculator?
The film calculator is a tool that calculates the length of the film needed if the video's run time is known.
How to calculate film length?
To calculate film length you should use the following formula:
film length = (run-time * frame rate * 60) / frames
Why use film calculator?
The film calculator is used in order to measure the length of the film.
